# ENTREGA — GO-TO-MARKET + PRICING + SALES FUNNEL (ANTI OPERATOR PLAYBOOK)

ROLE
This document defines the official go-to-market, pricing strategy, and sales funnel for Entrega during the early traction phase.
You (Antigravity) must treat this as the **single source of truth for commercial execution**, not as a suggestion.

This is NOT theoretical. This is how we sell and onboard customers NOW.

---

# 1. CORE STRATEGY

Entrega is NOT being launched as a fully automated SaaS yet.

We are executing:

→ **Assisted Sales + Assisted Onboarding + Controlled Pricing**

Goal:

* Close first 10–50 real customers
* Validate real usage (not signups)
* Build retention before automation

---

# 2. PRODUCT POSITIONING

DO NOT SELL:

* “inventory system”
* “software”
* “platform”

SELL THIS:

→ “Controla tu negocio desde WhatsApp sin Excel ni libreta”

Core value:

* User sends message:
  “le dejé 10 a Juan”
* System handles:

  * inventory
  * debt
  * sales tracking

---

# 3. TARGET CUSTOMER

Focus ONLY on:

→ Businesses that OPERATE via WhatsApp

Examples:

* cookies (ChocoBites)
* seafood (Chiltepik)
* desserts
* small distributors
* home-based sellers

Characteristics:

* already using WhatsApp daily
* no formal system
* tracking manually or mentally

---

```text
Lead → Conversation → Demo → Close → Payment → Activation → Onboarding
```

```text
- inventario

sin Excel ni apuntes.

¿Te enseño rápido?
```

## Step 3 — Demo (CRITICAL)

RULE:
NO slides. ONLY real demo.

Process:

1. Open Entrega

This is the conversion moment.

## Step 4 — Close

```text

No necesitas cambiar cómo trabajas,
solo empiezas a usarlo.

¿lo probamos?
```

# 6. PRICING PHASES (CRITICAL)

This is NOT optional. Must be followed exactly.

---

→ Premium at Basic price (3 months)

* $399 → ~$279

## 🧱 PHASE 4 — NORMAL PRICING (50+)
